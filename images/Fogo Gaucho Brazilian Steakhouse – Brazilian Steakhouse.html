Welcome to
Fogo Gaucho

Late 2007, native Brazilian Gaucho Chefs began sharing the centuries-old grilling tradition of churrasco in Nairobi.

Fogo Gaucho Churrascaria is Nairobi's only All You Can Eat Brazilian Churrascaria serving 17 cuts of meat and 25 different salads, sides and dessets every lunch and dinner.

Welcome to the Fogo Experience at our 2 branches located in Viking House, Westlands and Galana Plaza, Kilimani.
